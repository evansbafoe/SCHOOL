Wisdom Larry Quarme Doe

CEO/FOUNDER

Mr. Wisdom Larry Quarme Doe is the founder of National Juniors Challenge (NJC QUIZ & NJC KIDZ QUIZ). He also serves as the Chief Executive Officer of Uniwiz Foundation. He started NJC in 2019 and NJC Kidz Quiz in 2023.
He is a husband of one wife and blessed with four beautiful children. He holds a master's degree in Project Management and Management & Leardership from ACCRA BUSINESS SCHOOL (ABS) and PAN AFRICAN LEADERSHIP INSTITUTE (PALI) respectively. He also holds Bachelor's degree in Business Administration from Kwame Nkrumah University of Science and Technology (KNUST).
