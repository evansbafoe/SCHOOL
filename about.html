HOW WE STARTED

Since 2018, The National Juniors Challenge (NJC) has rapidly become a prominent force in the realm of quizzing, leaving an indelible mark on the intellectual landscape of the country. Dedicated to fostering knowledge and critical thinking, NJC has consistently organized quiz programs that span the entire nation, engaging participants of all ages and backgrounds. The company's commitment to promoting education and intellectual curiosity is exemplified through its flagship quiz programs, namely the National Junior Challenge Quiz (NJC QUIZ) and the National Junior Challenge KIDZ Quiz (NJC KDZ QUIZ).


The NJC QUIZ caters specifically for young minds, offering a platform for students to showcase their academic prowess and general knowledge. This initiative does not only encourages healthy competition but also instills the love for learning and exploration. On the other hand, the NJC QUIZ takes a broader approach, incorporating elements of knowledge discovery to challenge participants beyond conventional academic boundaries. Both quizzes have gained widespread popularity, drawing participants and audiences from diverse regions. As the NJC continues to evolve and expand its reach, it remains a dynamic force in shaping the intellectual landscape of the nation, fostering a culture of curiosity and lifelong learning.
