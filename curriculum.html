It is strongly recommended that teams and coaches watch past episodes of NJC Quiz on SOCIAL MEDIA HANDLES to become familiar with the format, pacing, and the types of questions common to The NJC QUIZ.

The Quiz would encompass FOUR subject areas to give a broad-based appreciation of diversity in knowledge. The main categories of the Quiz subject areas are with respect to Ghana, Africa and & the World in the following areas...

1. ICT:

Information Technology ,   Introduction to Computer ,  

2.MATHEMATICS

Arithmetic & Algebra  ,   Geometry & Mensuration  ,   Statistics & Probability  ,  Non-routine problem solving: number patterns, divisibility tests, logic problems

3. SCIENCE:

Health and Human Body  ,   Physical Science  ,   Life Science ,   Earth Science  ,   Space Science  

4. SOCIAL:

Ghanaian Heroes  ,   Current Affairs  ,   People and Places  ,   Economy  and   Country and World Facts
